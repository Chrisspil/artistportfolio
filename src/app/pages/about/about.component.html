About the Artist

Christos Spiliotis was born in the 1980s in an urban environment, but by choice, he now resides far from the noise of the city, in a peaceful place where mountain and sea coexist in harmony. Following his studies (2019–2022), he dedicated himself entirely to his artistic nature.

His work reflects both a restless spirit and a deeply expressive mind—qualities that permeate his creations. While a significant portion of his output is created digitally, it maintains the immediacy and soul of hand-drawn expression and traditional painting.

At 41, Spiliotis has established a strong, distinctive identity, confirming that his work is mature and competitive on the international stage.

Artistic Statement

My approach is Expressive Impressionism, centered on the thematic concept of Psychic Topography.

My work is defined by the immediacy of the gesture, the thick application of paint (impasto), and a dynamic dialogue of materials. My art focuses on the deconstruction of space or form, conveying internal emotional intensity rather than realistic representation.

Every subject transforms into a landscape of the soul:

  • Whether through internal spaces that capture memory or anticipation
  • Or through expressive portraits that function as commentary on my art (such as Picasso and Mona Lisa)

What remains is the power of emotion-yhe element that drives the evolution of he piece
